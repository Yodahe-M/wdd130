Contact and Location

Where are we located

We live in a little town outside of Salt Lake City, Utah, and there is no Ethiopian food nearby. Our website will make it easier for individuals in the area to order and experience Ethiopian food. They will have quick access to Ethiopian food and will not have to drive an hour or 30 minutes to enjoy it. We will provide our phone number, Instagram and Facebook account information. We share short videos of us creating food and others consuming food on our social media page. This will provide our consumers a sneak peek at how we cook our meals. People may sometimes tag us and share their experience with us, as well as give a review on the food. Because our food is largely vegan, our customers enjoy it. If you want a daily update, follow us on Instagram and social media. We occasionally have popups on Saturdays, which we promote on our social media accounts. People will visit my website to order Ethiopian cuisine. When someone owns a restaurant, it is critical that they have a website that allows customers to readily access their goods. Contact us for more information.
